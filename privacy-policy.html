Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Converso ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Converso mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Language preferences: your native language and the language you are learning.
  • Learning content: flashcard collections, vocabulary words, and chat messages you create within the App.
  • App settings: font size preferences and other personalization choices.

1.2 Information Generated by Your Use

  • Conversation history: messages exchanged with the AI language coach.
  • Flashcard performance data: your correct and incorrect answer counts per word.
  • Audio cache: temporary audio files synthesized for text-to-speech playback, stored locally on your device and in our secure cloud storage.
  • Usage metadata: timestamps and session data used to improve your learning experience.

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

  • We collect app usage events (such as app opens, lesson completions, minigame completions, and in-app purchases) via the Meta (Facebook) SDK. These events are shared with Meta Platforms, Inc. for ad measurement, audience building, and ad delivery optimization. Meta may use this data in accordance with its Data Policy (facebook.com/policy).
  • The App may show ads (e.g. via Google AdMob). Ad serving and related data are handled by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy) and Google's ad practices.
  • Our other third-party providers (e.g. Supabase, or the app store from which you downloaded the App) may process technical or device-related data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features.
  • Personalize your learning experience (e.g., generating flashcards and AI chat responses relevant to your language and level).
  • Synthesize and cache text-to-speech audio in the language you are learning.
  • Translate the App's user interface into your native language.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve and develop new features of the App.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

Converso uses the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy and terms of service:

Supabase

We use Supabase (supabase.com) for secure user authentication, database storage of your account data and learning content, and cloud storage of synthesized audio files. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified. Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure; the region depends on our project configuration (e.g. United States or other supported regions).

OpenAI

We use the OpenAI API (openai.com) to power the AI language coach, generate flashcard content, and translate the App interface. Your conversation messages and learning context are sent to OpenAI's servers to generate responses. OpenAI does not use data submitted via the API to train its models by default. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy at openai.com/privacy.

Google Cloud Text-to-Speech

We use the Google Cloud Text-to-Speech API (cloud.google.com) to synthesize audio pronunciations of words and phrases. Text you request to hear is sent to Google's servers for synthesis. Please review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Meta (Facebook) SDK

We use the Meta SDK to log app usage events (such as app launches, lesson completions, minigame completions, and purchases) and share them with Meta Platforms, Inc. for advertising measurement, audience building, and ad campaign optimization. Meta may associate these events with your Meta account or device. You can review Meta's Data Policy at facebook.com/policy and manage your ad preferences at facebook.com/adpreferences.

Google AdMob

We may show ads in the App via Google AdMob. Google may collect or use data for ad delivery and measurement in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and ad settings. You can manage ad preferences at adssettings.google.com. Subscribers to Converso Premium do not see ads.

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we will ask for your consent before showing personalized ads. You can review or withdraw your ad consent at any time via Settings → Privacy & cookie settings within the App.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Synthesized audio files are cached to reduce API costs and improve performance; they are retained indefinitely until you delete your account or we purge the cache.

If you delete your account, we permanently delete all of your personal data, including your profile, conversation history, flashcard collections, and any associated audio cache, within 30 days. Account deletion is irreversible.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings → Delete Account within the App.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the feedback form below. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Converso is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete the information.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage, secure HTTPS transmission, and server-side API key management. AI and TTS API keys are never exposed to the client app. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our third-party service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to such transfers. We take appropriate steps to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to: know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose; request deletion of your personal information; and not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell your personal information. We share app usage events with Meta Platforms, Inc. for advertising purposes as described in Sections 1.3 and 3. To make a request or opt out, contact us via the feedback form below.

10. International Users (EU/EEA/UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under applicable data protection law (e.g. GDPR):

  • Legal basis: We process your data where necessary to perform our contract with you (providing the App), for our legitimate interests (improving the App, security), or with your consent where required.
  • You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country.
  • When we transfer your data outside the UK/EEA, we rely on adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, or other approved transfer mechanisms.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and, where required by law, by sending you a notification. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
